Welcome to TessaLectio OCR website

Welcome to our OCR website. The purpose of this website is to serve as a user interface for our OCR server.

OCR is an acronym for Optical Character Recognition and the technology is primarily used to convert images of documents or text into a workable format that you can manipulate afterwards with a computer. Our application is using the Tesseract OCR originally developed by HP for use in their printers. Allthough it isn't the most accurate OCR by todays standards, Tesseract was considered one if the top OCR engines in 2006.

The engine was quite ahead of its time considering the fact that it stayed among the top contenders for almost 15 years!
